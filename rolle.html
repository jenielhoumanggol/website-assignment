Santiago M. Rolle Hall

It is a big hall named after Santiago M. Rolle, the University’s First President. It can accommodate 250 people. It is suitable for wedding receptions, birthdays, seminar and workshops, political events etc.

Some Information

Santiago M. Rolle Hall
Location: Cavite State University - Main Campus, Indang, Cavite, 4122 Philippines.
Capacity: Accommodates up to 250 people.
Events: Hosts university activities, including orientations and general assemblies.
