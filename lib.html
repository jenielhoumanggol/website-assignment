Ladislao N. Diwa Memorial Library and Museum

Brief History: The two-storey University Library started its operation in June 1980, with a collection of 2,946 volumes (consisting of 1,196 titles) turned over from the old high school library. After the books were catalogued, the library finally opened its door to the users in November 1980.
